Italeri Spitfire Mk.V / Mk.IX Aces

First kit of 2026 started! I was technically working on a B1 lancer that i started late 2025, but I paused (scrapped, realistically) that project for now.

This project started with me wanting to build the Swedish S31 reconnaissance aircraft. Sweden purchased some Spitfire Mk. XIX after WW2. As you might have realized, a Mk. VB and Mk. XIX is not the same.I had misread XIX as IX, and did not realize until I started writing a blog post about the build. I guess I will have to see this as a trial run until I can get my hands on a XIX kit
